About Ms. King

Ms. Larica King has loved art since childhood and is eager to show the next generation her passion. Ms. King loves children and will not push, she desires to simply lead and promote children's passion. Although Ms. King's students compete in competitions, Ms. King does not operate a competitive classroom, but instead teaches good manners, to both winner and loser.

Looking Back

Ms. King has taught art to children of all ages for a decade, and her love for it has not diminished. She continues to shower her pupils with love and determination. Throughout the years, many children have enjoyed taking her class and learning from her, while also having fun!
